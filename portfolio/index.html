Who am I


First things first: I am a web developer and designer based in Jerusalem,Israel. While most people specialize in either development or design, I am one of those unique people that sees value in mastering both fields. Without a good design, good development will be difficult if not outright impossible. Without good development, a good design likewise cannot achieve it's fullest potential.
I am at the intersection between making things happen and asking the deeper philosophical questions such as:
  • What makes a good website?
  • Why are some designs and layouts more aesthetic than others?
  • Is typography and font the difference between looking mundane and being truly awesome?
  • Are my pictures doing justice to the message I want to present to the world?
